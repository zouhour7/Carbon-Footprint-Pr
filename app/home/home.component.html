​Carbon FootPrint

Say - what is carbon FootPrint?



​ ​A carbon footprint is a measure of the amount of carbon dioxide (CO2e) and other greenhouse gases released into the atmosphere as a result of human activities,
such as burning fossil fuels for energy, transportation, and various industrial processes.
It quantifies the impact of these activities on climate change by estimating the total emissions of greenhouse gases associated with a particular individual/product.
